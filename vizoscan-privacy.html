PREMIUM DOCUMENT SUITE

Last Updated: October 24, 2024

At VizoScan, we believe your private documents should stay private. Our "Golden Rule" is simple: Your document data never leaves your device.

1. 100% On-Device Processing

VizoScan uses Google ML Kit and high-performance local engines to process your scans and documents. All OCR (Text Recognition) and PDF generation happen offline on your device hardware. We do not use cloud servers to process, view, or store your documents.

2. Universal File Handling

VizoScan is designed to manage PDF, Word, Excel, and PPT files. When you view these files, the app uses secure local intents. We do not index the content of your files for any purpose other than displaying them to you within the app.

3. Modern Permissions & Security

  • Camera: Used only when you trigger a "Scan". Images are processed in memory and never uploaded.
  • System Photo Picker: We use the secure Android Photo Picker. This ensures we only have access to the specific images you explicitly select.
  • Storage: We save files to the Documents/VizoScan folder. This follows Scoped Storage rules to keep your data organized and secure.

4. Data Safety Compliance

To support app improvements, we may use anonymized analytics (Firebase/Google Analytics). This data includes app crashes and feature usage statistics but never includes your document names, content, or personal files.
