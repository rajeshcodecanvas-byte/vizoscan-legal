Last Updated: April 23, 2026

Welcome to VizoScan. We believe your private documents should stay private. Our "Golden Rule" is simple: Your data never leaves your device.

1. On-Device AI Processing

VizoScan uses Google ML Kit and local PDF engines to process your scans. All text recognition (OCR) and PDF generation happen 100% offline on your hardware. We do not operate any cloud servers that see or store your documents.

2. Modern Permissions

  • Camera: Used only when you trigger a "Scan" to capture images.
  • Photo Picker: We use the Android System Photo Picker for "Image to PDF". This is the most secure method and grants us access ONLY to the specific images you choose.
  • Storage: We save your generated PDFs to the public Documents/VizoScan folder so you can access them in other apps.

3. Transparency & Third-Parties

To keep the app free and professional, we use:

  • Firebase/Google Analytics: To see which features are popular (anonymized).
  • AdMob: To show occasional ads. No document data is ever shared with advertisers.
