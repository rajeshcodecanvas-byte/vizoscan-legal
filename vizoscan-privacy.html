Welcome to VizoScan: AI Document Reader. Your privacy is our highest priority. This policy reinforces our commitment to On-Device Privacy—meaning your documents stay on your phone, where they belong.

1. Local Data Processing

VizoScan is built with a "Privacy-First" architecture. All core features—including PDF scanning, image-to-PDF conversion, and document reading—are performed locally on your device. We do not transmit your documents or images to external servers for processing.

2. Permissions & Transparency

To provide a world-class experience, VizoScan requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture high-quality document scans. Images are processed in real-time and are never stored outside the app's secure internal storage.
  • Media/Storage Access: To index and display your PDF, Word, Excel, and PPT files for convenient reading.
  • Internet: Required solely for analytics, crash reporting, and serving non-intrusive advertisements.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to maintain app quality and support development:

  • Google AdMob: For personalized advertising. You can manage your ad preferences via your Google Account settings.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For anonymized crash reporting to ensure the app remains stable and professional.

4. Data Retention

Because we do not collect your personal data or document contents, we do not have a retention period for such information. Any files created within the app are stored in your device's local memory and are deleted if you clear the app data or uninstall the application.
